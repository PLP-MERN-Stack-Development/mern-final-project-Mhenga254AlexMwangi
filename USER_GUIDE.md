## üöÄ Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click **"Register"** in the navigation bar
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for verification (if implemented)

### Logging In
1. Click **"Login"** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

## üìù Creating Your First Recipe

### Step 1: Access Recipe Creation
- Click **"Create Recipe"** in the navigation bar (must be logged in)
- Or click the **"+"** button if available on mobile

### Step 2: Fill Recipe Details
- **Title**: Give your recipe a catchy name
- **Description**: Describe what makes your recipe special
- **Prep Time**: How long it takes to prepare ingredients
- **Cook Time**: How long it takes to cook
- **Servings**: Number of people it serves
- **Difficulty**: Easy, Medium, or Hard

### Step 3: Add Ingredients
- Click **"Add Ingredient"** for each ingredient
- Be specific with measurements (e.g., "2 cups flour")
- List ingredients in the order they're used

### Step 4: Write Instructions
- Click **"Add Step"** for each instruction
- Be clear and sequential
- Include cooking times and temperatures
- Add tips and variations if you have them

### Step 5: Upload Images
- Click **"Choose Files"** to select images
- **Process Images**: Show cooking steps
- **Final Result**: Mark one image as the finished dish
- Add captions to explain each image
- Maximum 5 images, 10MB each

### Step 6: Add Tags
- Enter relevant tags separated by commas
- Examples: "vegetarian, italian, quick, healthy"
- Tags help others discover your recipe

### Step 7: Publish
- Click **"Create Recipe"** to publish
- Your recipe is now live and visible to all users!
